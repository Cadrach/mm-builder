How it works

This is a deck builder for the game Minion Masters.
The aim of this tool is to allow people to quickly create & share decks for the game using a simple URL.

  • Adding a card works exactly like in the game: click on it to have it put at the top in your deck.
  • To remove card simply click on it on your deck
  • Hover over a card for more information
  • To share a deck you can click on the  Share button at the top, or simply copy the URL of the browser.


Additional notes

This is not an official application.

The data is not automatically refreshed. Currently, the freshness of the data will depend on the game's developers ability to maintain a definition file in the future.

Currently, your decks are not saved anywhere, the URL is the only means of saving.

The statistics block is informational, and try to capture a few simple things.
